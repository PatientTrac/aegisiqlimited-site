Acceptable Use Policy

Rules for using AegisIQ Wealth Ledger responsibly and lawfully.

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Permitted Use

Use the platform only for lawful accounting, record-keeping, and wealth-tracking purposes for entities and individuals you are authorized to represent.

Prohibited Conduct

  • Using the platform to record, conceal, or facilitate fraud, money laundering, tax evasion, or other unlawful activity.
  • Uploading content you do not have the right to use, or that infringes others' rights.
  • Attempting to breach security, access other tenants' data, or defeat entity-level isolation.
  • Reverse engineering, scraping, or overloading the platform, or circumventing usage limits.
  • Misrepresenting your identity or authority, or sharing credentials with unauthorized persons.

Enforcement

We may investigate suspected violations and may suspend or terminate access as described in the Terms of Service. We may report unlawful activity to authorities where required.
