Product combinations

Use one application—or connect responsibilities across the lifecycle.

These combinations describe operating fit. They do not imply that every cross-product integration is already live; implementation status belongs in each product roadmap.

Equity + Wealth Ledger

Carry investment research assumptions into portfolio review and valuation workflows while keeping the accounting record separately governed.

Stock Ledger + Investor Portal

Coordinate investor activity and post-close ownership administration without treating a portal submission as an automatic ledger change.

Stock Ledger + Wealth Ledger

Relate ownership interests and private-company values to an owner or group reporting view while preserving legal and accounting boundaries.

All four applications

Support research, ownership, accounting, and capital operations under one company’s governance principles and product-family experience.
