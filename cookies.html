Corporate Cookie Notice

How cookies and similar technologies may be used on the AegisIQ Limited corporate website.

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Legal CenterPrivacyWebsite TermsCookiesDisclaimerAcceptable UseProduct Refunds

1. Scope

This notice applies to aegisiqlimited.com. Product applications may use different cookies and must publish their own cookie notices and consent controls.

2. What cookies are

Cookies and similar technologies store or access information on a browser or device. They can support essential website functions, remember preferences, help detect abuse, and—when enabled—measure website performance.

3. Categories

  • Strictly necessary: security, network delivery, consent records, and core website operation.
  • Preferences: choices such as language or display preferences.
  • Analytics: aggregated or pseudonymous measurements used only when the applicable consent and configuration requirements are satisfied.

4. No product-account cookies

The corporate site does not itself create a Wealth Ledger, Stock Ledger, Equity, or Investor Portal account. Cookies used after visiting a product site are governed by that product’s notice.

5. Managing choices

You can use any on-site consent control and your browser settings to manage cookies. Blocking strictly necessary technologies may prevent portions of the website from functioning.

6. Cookie inventory

The production cookie inventory must be kept consistent with the scripts actually deployed. AegisIQ Limited should update this notice before enabling a new analytics, advertising, session-replay, or marketing technology.
