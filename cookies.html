What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences (including your language selection), secure the service, and understand usage.

Types We Use

  • Strictly necessary — authentication, security, and core functionality.
  • Preferences — settings such as language and display choices.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect functionality such as staying signed in.
