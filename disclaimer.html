Software and Informational Outputs Only

AegisIQ Wealth Ledger provides accounting and record-keeping software and informational outputs only. It does not provide accounting, audit, tax, legal, investment, or financial advice, and is not a substitute for a qualified professional.

Not a Substitute for a Professional

Outputs — including ledgers, reports, consolidations, categorizations, valuations, and net-worth figures — are generated from the data you provide and are intended to support, not replace, the judgment of a qualified accountant, auditor, tax adviser, or attorney. You should obtain professional advice before filing, reporting, or making decisions based on the platform.

Accuracy of Data and Outputs

The platform depends on the accuracy and completeness of the data you enter or import, including opening balances and historical records. Automated and AI-assisted categorization and reconciliation may contain errors and must be reviewed. We do not warrant that outputs are accurate, complete, or fit for a particular reporting or tax purpose.

No Fiduciary or Advisory Relationship

Use of the platform does not create a fiduciary, agency, accountant-client, or advisory relationship between you and AegisIQ Limited.

Multi-Jurisdiction Note

Accounting standards, tax rules, and reporting requirements differ by jurisdiction and entity type. You are responsible for determining and meeting the requirements applicable to each of your entities.
