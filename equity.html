Operating workflow

A controlled path from input to decision.

The workflow is designed to preserve context, require the right review, and leave a traceable record of material actions.

Start with a security

Enter a ticker, company, or saved research target and choose the analytical workflow.

Assemble the evidence

AI and market-data workflows organize fundamentals, catalysts, technical context, and valuation inputs.

Review the thesis

Analysts inspect assumptions, confirmation, risks, and source context before approval.

Publish with controls

Export a governed report, retain the review history, and revisit the thesis as facts change.
