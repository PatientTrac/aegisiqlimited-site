Security & compliance

Governance is the default, not a feature.

The name is a promise. Every AegisIQ product inherits the same controls, so trust never resets when you move between them.

01 / Isolation

Entity-scoped by design

Role-based access with entity-level isolation across every product. Your US corporation and your UK entity stay walled apart — separation enforced in the data model, not just the UI.

02 / Encryption

Bank-grade security

AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit protect sensitive ownership and financial data end to end, on one shared security spine.

03 / Record

Immutable & attested

Every action is logged with a timestamp and user attribution. Views show an as-of timestamp and last-attestation date, so you always know the currency of what you're reading.
