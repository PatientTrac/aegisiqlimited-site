Capabilities

What AegisIQ Investor Portal is designed to do.

The corporate overview explains the product boundary. The product site contains the live application, current plan details, demonstrations, and product-specific disclosures.

OB

Investor onboarding

Invite authorized users, establish profiles, and guide each participant through the required steps.

DR

Controlled document room

Publish versioned offering materials and restrict access by investor, role, offering, or status.

QL

Qualification review

Collect relevant representations and route exceptions to authorized reviewers.

SB

Subscription workflow

Coordinate documents, signatures, amounts, status, and completeness checks.

AP

Issuer approvals

Separate investor submission from issuer acceptance and retain the authorized decision history.

CM

Communications

Maintain controlled notices, reminders, and activity history around each investor relationship.

CL

Closing operations

Track commitments, approvals, funding status, and completion milestones across the offering.

AU

Audit trail

Retain version, access, action, reviewer, and timestamp context for material workflow events.

Operating workflow

A controlled path from input to decision.

The workflow is designed to preserve context, require the right review, and leave a traceable record of material actions.

Review subscriptions

Coordinate closing
