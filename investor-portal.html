From interest to close, on one trail.

The issuer and investor operations layer — and the identity backbone of the network.

AegisIQ Investor Portal lets issuers qualify investors, share offering documents, run subscription workflows, and manage approvals from one secure portal. Built for private placements and Reg S international offerings, it keeps a clear, auditable trail from an investor's first interest through subscription close — and provides the email and identity program the whole network runs on.

On the network

One entity, one record.

The Investor Portal closes the capital continuum. The entities researched in Equity, owned in Stock Ledger, and measured in Wealth Ledger become the entities you finance here — and every completed subscription writes back to the same entity_id, so ownership updates flow to the cap table and the loop begins again.
