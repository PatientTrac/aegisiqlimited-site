Corporate Cookie Notice

How cookies and similar technologies may be used on the AegisIQ Limited corporate website.

Effective date: 14 July 2026 · Last updated: 14 July 2026 · Version 1.0

Legal CenterPrivacyWebsite TermsCookiesDisclaimerAcceptable UseProduct Refunds

1. Scope

This notice applies to aegisiqgroup.com. Product applications may use different cookies and must publish their own cookie notices and consent controls.

2. Cookies and similar technologies

Cookies are small files stored by your browser. “Similar technologies” include browser-storage mechanisms such as localStorage and sessionStorage. Those storage mechanisms are technically distinct from cookies — they are not transmitted with every request and are not shared across sites — but this notice describes both for completeness.

3. What this website currently uses

The corporate website is a static, informational site. As deployed, it:

  • sets no cookies;
  • uses no localStorage or sessionStorage;
  • loads no analytics, advertising, session-replay, social, or marketing technologies, and no third-party trackers;
  • loads only first-party static assets (stylesheets, a small navigation script, images, and icons) served from this domain.

4. Cookie and storage inventory

No cookies or browser-storage entries are set by this website.

If a preference feature such as a language selector is enabled in future, it would store a first-party preference value (for example aegisiq_lang) in localStorage. That value is browser storage, not a cookie, and is not in use at this time. This notice and this table will be updated before any new cookie, analytics, advertising, session-replay, or marketing technology is enabled.

5. No product-account cookies

The corporate site does not itself create a Wealth Ledger, Stock Ledger, Equity, or Investor Portal account. Each product application maintains its own cookie notice and consent controls; technologies used after you visit a product site are governed by that product’s notice.

6. Managing your browser

Because this website sets no cookies and uses no browser storage, no on-site consent banner is required. You can still clear or block cookies and site data through your browser settings at any time; product applications provide their own consent controls where applicable.
