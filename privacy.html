Privacy Policy

How AegisIQ Wealth Ledger collects, uses, shares, stores, and protects information across accounts, entity books, imports, connected accounts, and billing.

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Information We Collect

We may collect the categories of information below to operate the platform.

  • Account and authentication details (name, email, organization, credentials).
  • Entity and workspace content — the business entities, personal or household books, chart of accounts, and ledgers you create.
  • Financial records you import or enter — transactions, balances, invoices, bills, and reconciliations.
  • Connected-account metadata where you enable bank, brokerage, or accounting-system connections through third-party aggregators.
  • Uploaded files and documents, including receipts stored in the document vault.
  • Billing and subscription records handled through our payment provider.
  • Support, sales, and demo submissions.
  • Technical data such as device, browser, log, and cookie data.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide and maintain the platform; authenticate users; import, categorize, reconcile, and consolidate financial records; process billing; respond to requests; secure the service and prevent abuse; improve reliability; and comply with legal obligations.

AI Processing and User Content

AegisIQ Wealth Ledger may process transactions, uploaded materials, and related content through automated and AI-assisted systems or service providers to deliver requested functionality such as categorization and reconciliation support. You are responsible for reviewing outputs before relying on them for accounting, tax, or reporting purposes.

Financial Data and Connected Accounts

Where you connect external bank, brokerage, or accounting sources, connection and transaction data is retrieved through third-party aggregators subject to their terms and the permissions you grant. You may disconnect a source at any time; previously imported records remain in your workspace unless you delete them.

Sharing and Sub-Processors

We may share information with vendors that support hosting, authentication, payments, data aggregation, email delivery, AI processing, customer support, and security monitoring. We require these providers to protect information consistent with this policy. We do not sell personal information to advertisers.

International Data Transfers

AegisIQ Limited is incorporated in Hong Kong and serves users in multiple countries, including the United States, the United Kingdom, the European Union, and Colombia. Information may be processed in, and transferred across, jurisdictions with different data-protection laws. Where required, we rely on appropriate legal transfer mechanisms and safeguards.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, or restrict the processing of your personal information, and to object to certain processing or withdraw consent. These may arise under Hong Kong's Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance, the EU/UK GDPR, Colombia's Ley 1581 de 2012, the California Consumer Privacy Act (as amended), and other laws.

To exercise a right, contact legal@aegisiqfintech.com. We may need to verify your identity, and some records may be retained where required for security, billing, compliance, audit, or legal reasons.

Security and Retention

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information, including encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256) and role-based, entity-level access isolation. No system can be guaranteed perfectly secure.

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service, satisfy legal and tax obligations, resolve disputes, maintain audit records, and enforce agreements.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies as described in our Cookie Policy.

Children

The platform is intended for business and adult personal use and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date and, where appropriate, additional notice.
