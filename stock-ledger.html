Operating workflow

A controlled path from input to decision.

The workflow is designed to preserve context, require the right review, and leave a traceable record of material actions.

Establish the security structure

Create entities, stock classes, authorized amounts, instruments, and stakeholder records.

Initiate a governed action

Prepare an issuance, transfer, certificate action, split, or cancellation with supporting evidence.

Review and approve

Route the action through the required approval chain and resolve exceptions before execution.

Lock and report

Update the ownership record, retain the audit history, and generate as-of reports for stakeholders.
