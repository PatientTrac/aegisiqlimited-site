Why it’s different

Five things most accounting software makes you give up.

AegisIQ closes the exact gaps operators hit in QuickBooks and Xero once a business gets real.

MX

Multi-entity by design

Unlimited entities, intercompany that balances both sides, and true consolidation with foreign-currency translation. vs. QuickBooks bills a separate subscription per company; neither it nor Xero consolidates natively.

OW

Owner & wealth intelligence

S-corp owner-expense differential, shareholder basis, valuation, ownership-adjusted net worth, and personal & household books. vs. Not what the incumbents are built to do.

FD

Keeps the full detail

Intake never drops a source column; corporate meals carry attendees, purpose, and jurisdiction-aware deductibility; unlimited custom dimensions. vs. QuickBooks caps custom fields at 1–12 by plan.

IG

Compliance-grade integrity

Atomic exactly-once posting, immutable entries with reversals, period locks, attested fiscal-year changes, approvals, and an evidence vault. vs. Editable entries and lighter controls elsewhere.

ST

The whole accounting stack

Perpetual inventory (FIFO & weighted-average), fixed assets, prepaids/accruals, revenue recognition, loans, budgets, and treasury — native, not add-ons. vs. Higher tiers or third-party add-ons elsewhere.
