Capabilities

What AegisIQ Wealth Ledger does.

Consolidation

One net-worth view

Assets and liabilities across every entity, currency, and jurisdiction roll up into a single, reconciled net-worth picture.

Imports

Clean data in

Import full history from QuickBooks and other accounting systems, with reconciliation checks on record counts, dates, and closing balances.

Traceability

Source to figure

Every number traces back to the entity and transaction it came from, so the roll-up is auditable, not a black box.

Currency

Correct historical FX

Multi-currency holdings convert to one reporting currency at transaction-date rates, so years of history read the way they actually happened.

Isolation

Books stay separate

Entity isolation keeps corporate and personal books walled apart, across jurisdictions, while still rolling into the combined view.

Openings

Partial-history aware

Opening-balance handling captures positions that predate your accounting data, so the ledger never starts an entity mid-stream.

On the network

One entity, one record.

Wealth Ledger measures what the network holds. It draws ownership valuations from Stock Ledger and can reflect research context from Equity, then presents the consolidated position that informs fundraising in the Investor Portal. One entity_id per entity means the wealth view is always the same entity you research, own, and finance elsewhere.
