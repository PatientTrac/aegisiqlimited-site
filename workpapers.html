Governance invariants

Three rules the software will not break — for anyone.

These aren't settings. They're hard-coded invariants, because a review file is only as trustworthy as the controls around it.

Invariant 01

DRAFT until sign-off

Every export carries a DRAFT watermark until the engagement CPA records sign-off. Blocking open items close the export gate entirely — an incomplete file physically cannot leave clean.

Invariant 02

The software never issues the report

AegisIQ Workpapers organizes evidence and tracks conclusions. The review report is authored, judged, and issued by the licensed practitioner — never generated on their behalf.

Invariant 03

Branding is cosmetic only

Firms can apply their logo, letterhead, and colors to engagement packages. Branding can never alter assurance language, watermarks, or gate behavior.

How it works

One engagement, six governed steps.

The pipeline mirrors how a review file is actually built — and won't let a later step outrun an earlier one.

Engagement

Set the client, fiscal year, standard, and basis of accounting. Equity articulation is tracked from day one.

Sources

Attach bank data, trial balances, and management-adopted statements as engagement evidence.

Tie-out

Line-by-line per-books vs. per-ledger comparison. Every line is tied, a variance, a gap, or a flag — with a note.

Statements

Track the financial statements management adopts, and whether they articulate exactly.

Open items

Numbered items with owner and severity. Blocking items hold the export gate closed until resolved.

Package

Assemble the workpaper package under the firm's letterhead — DRAFT-watermarked until CPA sign-off.

Features

Built for the practitioner running the file.

Live ledger tie-outs

Per-books figures reference Wealth Ledger's double-entry records directly, so a tie-out never argues with a stale export.

Four-state line status

Tied, variance, gap, flag — a vocabulary strict enough to be auditable and simple enough to read at a glance.

Open items with teeth

Owner, severity, and status per item. "Blocking" means blocking: the package won't finalize around it.

Equity articulation tracking

Opening equity, net income, distributions, closing equity — carried across fiscal years so year two opens exactly where year one closed.

Firm-branded packages

Your letterhead, logo, and colors on every engagement package — with governance language locked beyond reach.

Multi-engagement clients

Run FY2024 and FY2025 side by side for the same client, with framework-consistency items tracked between them.
